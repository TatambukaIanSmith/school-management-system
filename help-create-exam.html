Creating a New Exam

Complete guide to setting up exams for your students

1

Navigate to Exams Page

Click on "Exams" in the sidebar menu, then click the "Create Exam" button at the top of the page.

2

Fill in Exam Details

Provide the basic information about your exam:

  • Exam Title: Clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Mathematics Midterm 2026")
  • Subject: The subject area being tested
  • Duration: Time limit in minutes
  • Total Marks: Maximum possible score
  • Passing Score: Minimum score to pass
  • Instructions: Guidelines for students
3

Set Schedule

Define when students can take the exam:

Start Date & Time

When the exam becomes available

End Date & Time

Deadline for submission

4

Add Questions

Create your exam questions. Supported question types:

Multiple Choice

Students select one correct answer from options

True/False

Simple binary choice questions

Short Answer

Text-based responses requiring manual grading

Essay

Long-form answers with detailed responses

5

Assign to Students

Choose who can take the exam:

  • Select specific students individually
  • Assign to entire classes or grades
  • Use filters to target specific groups
6

Review and Publish

Double-check all details, then click "Publish Exam". Students will be notified and can access it during the scheduled time.

✓ Success: Your exam is now live and students can begin taking it during the scheduled period.

Best Practices

  • ✓ Test your exam before publishing by taking it yourself
  • ✓ Provide clear, unambiguous instructions
  • ✓ Set realistic time limits based on question count
  • ✓ Include a mix of question types for comprehensive assessment
  • ✓ Notify students in advance about upcoming exams
  • ✓ Save drafts frequently while creating questions
