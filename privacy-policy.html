Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how School Registration System ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our student management platform. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Email address and phone number
  • Employee or student ID
  • Role and access level
  • Academic records and exam results

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our platform, including browser type, IP address, and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To manage student registrations and records
  • To facilitate exam creation and grading
  • To communicate with you about your account
  • To improve our platform and user experience
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage technology. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With authorized school personnel based on role permissions

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in CSV format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for educational institutions and may be used by students of various ages. We take special care to protect the privacy of minors. Parents and guardians have the right to review, modify, or delete their child's information by contacting their school administrator.

Cookies and Tracking

We use localStorage to store session information and user preferences. This technology helps us provide a better user experience by remembering your settings and login status. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
