First Time Login Guide

Learn how to access your account and navigate the dashboard

Logging In

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Sign In"

Understanding Your Dashboard

After logging in, you'll see your personalized dashboard with:

  • Statistics: Quick overview of key numbers
  • Quick Actions: Common tasks you can perform
  • Navigation: Sidebar and navbar for easy access
  • Profile Menu: Click your avatar to manage profiles

Navigation Tips

Use the Sidebar

Access main sections like Dashboard, Students, Exams, and Users

Back/Forward Buttons

Use navigation buttons at the top of each page

Profile Dropdown

Click your avatar to switch profiles or sign out
