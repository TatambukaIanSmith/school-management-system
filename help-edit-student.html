Editing Student Information

Learn how to update and modify student records

1

Find the Student

Go to the Students page and locate the student you want to edit. You can:

  • Use the search bar to find by name or ID
  • Filter by class or grade level
  • Scroll through the student list
2

Open Edit Mode

Click on the student's row or the "Edit" button (pencil icon) next to their name. This will open the edit form with their current information.

3

Update Information

Modify any of the following fields as needed:

Personal Details

  • • Full Name
  • • Date of Birth
  • • Contact Number
  • • Email Address

Academic Details

  • • Student ID
  • • Class/Grade
  • • Enrollment Status
  • • Address
4

Save Changes

After making your changes, click the "Save" or "Update" button. The system will validate your changes and update the record.

💡 Tip: Changes are saved immediately and will be reflected across the entire system.

What You Can Edit

✓ Editable Fields

Name, contact info, class, address, and most personal details can be updated anytime.

⚠️ Restricted Fields

Student ID and email may be restricted depending on your role and system settings.

✗ Cannot Edit

Registration date, exam history, and system-generated data cannot be manually changed.

Permission Requirements

Different roles have different editing permissions:

Admin Full editing access to all fields
Registrar Can edit all student information
Secretary Can edit most fields except sensitive data
Bursar Can edit financial and contact information
